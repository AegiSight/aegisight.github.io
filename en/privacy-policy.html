Privacy Policy

Developer: Lumina Dev
Website: https://lumina-dev-lab.github.io/umrah-and-haji/

This Privacy Policy explains how Umrah & Hajj Companion handles information when you use the mobile application on iOS and Android. The app is developed by Lumina Dev.

1. Information We Handle

  • The app is designed to be offline-first. Most information you create is stored on your device.
  • On-device information may include ritual progress, Tawaf and Sa’i counter sessions, saved du’a, checklist items, saved map pins, emergency contacts, group safety details, language, text size, theme, offline pack status, purchase status, and coffee-support history.
  • If you use map, Qibla, or “my location” features, the app may request location permission to calculate direction, show your position, save pins, or help you orient yourself.
  • If you use coffee-support QR transfer features, the app may request camera access to scan QR codes.
  • If you make an in-app purchase, app stores and purchase services may process transaction information. The app may store purchase status and anonymous supporter identifiers to restore or verify purchases.
  • Advertising consent and ad delivery information may be handled through Google AdMob and Google User Messaging Platform where available.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide ritual guidance, offline content, counters, du’a, checklists, maps, Qibla, emergency organization, and other app features.
  • To save your preferences and progress locally on your device.
  • To show maps, saved places, offline map packs, and location-based orientation when you grant permission.
  • To support in-app purchases, ad removal, coffee-support history, and optional transfer of support history between devices.
  • To display advertising only in approved non-ritual surfaces and to respect available ad-consent choices.

3. Local Storage

The app uses local device storage, including Isar, SharedPreferences, secure storage, and files for offline packs. Local data remains on your device unless you share it, transfer it, use an optional configured backend feature, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

  • The app may use Google AdMob for ads, Google User Messaging Platform for consent choices, Mapbox for maps and offline map resources, app store purchase systems for in-app purchases, and optional backend services such as REST APIs or Supabase for coffee-support verification and transfer features when configured.
  • These services may process data under their own privacy policies.

5. Location, Camera, and Sharing

Location access is optional and can be changed in your device settings. Camera access is used for QR scanning only when you choose to scan. Native share actions may send meeting point, saved pin, hotel, bus, camp, guide, or similar details to the app or person you select.

6. Advertising

Ads may appear on non-sacred, non-active ritual surfaces such as Home or Profile/Tools areas. The app is designed not to show ads during active Tawaf or Sa’i counting sessions or interrupt ritual flows.

7. Children

The app is not designed specifically for children. If you believe information was provided inappropriately, contact us.

8. Retention and Deletion

Locally stored data remains until you delete it in the app, remove offline packs, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Purchase records may also be retained by app stores or optional backend services according to their policies.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact machealmaxin83@gmail.com.
