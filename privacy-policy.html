Umrah & Hajj Companion

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Umrah & Hajj Companion handles information when you use the mobile application on iOS and Android.

1. Information We Handle

Umrah & Hajj Companion is designed to work primarily on-device.

  • On-device content and settings, such as ritual progress, counters, saved du'a, checklists, offline packs, custom map pins, emergency contacts, group information, font size, dark mode, and language preferences.
  • Location data only when you grant permission for map features, current location display, or saved meeting point support.
  • Advertising and consent data through Google AdMob and Google User Messaging Platform, where applicable.
  • Map usage data through Mapbox when map tiles, map styles, or offline map packs are requested.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide ritual guidance and offline-first utility features.
  • To save your local preferences and app progress on your device.
  • To show maps, saved places, and offline map packs.
  • To display advertising only in approved, low-risk app surfaces.
  • To honor privacy and ad-consent choices where such choices are supported.

3. Local Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This may include ritual state, counters, map pins, offline pack metadata, checklists, and emergency information you choose to save.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may rely on the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for advertising in approved app screens.
  • Google User Messaging Platform for ad-consent and privacy-choice flows where applicable.
  • Mapbox for map display, map styles, and offline map region downloads.

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Location Permission

If you grant location access, the app may use your current GPS location to show where you are on the map, help orient you to saved places, and support route-guidance features. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device settings.

6. Advertising

Umrah & Hajj Companion is designed so ads do not interrupt sacred ritual flows or time-sensitive safety tasks. Ad-related preferences may be managed in the app where available.

7. Data Sharing

We do not describe this app as providing a user account or cloud sync service at this time. Data you create is primarily stored on your own device unless a third-party SDK processes limited technical data as part of map or advertising functionality.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete it in the app, remove offline packs, clear app data, or uninstall the app.

9. Children

Umrah & Hajj Companion is not designed specifically for children. If you believe personal information was provided inappropriately, please use the contact method below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted at this page with an updated effective date.
